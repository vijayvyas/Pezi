ABOUT THE PROGRAM


We are offers free, 8-week bootcamps for qualified applicants. Our curriculum is based around real-world examples, and has been tested by thousands of engineers from various companies.

Student learn by training, group discussions and pair programming exercises. Along with the 5-15 hours per week homework assignments which is designed to feature the most common use cases.

Eventually, students work in groups on a final product, which they present on the Product Demo event, in front of a few hundred people, for a Grand Prize Award, judged by local tech luminaries.

Our judges are executives from the reputed corporates and founders, established investors, and finalists.
