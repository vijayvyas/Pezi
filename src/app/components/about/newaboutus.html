Our Program


We offers free, 8-week bootcamps for qualified applicants with roadmap, a schedule, and goals. Our curriculum is based around real-world examples, and has been tested by thousands of engineers from various companies.

Train them by group discussions, pair programming exercises and 5-15 hours per week homework assignments which is designed to feature the most common use cases.

Eventually, students has to work in groups to complete a final product, which they present on the Product Demo event, in front of a few hundred people, for a Grand Prize Award, judged by local tech luminaries.

Our judges are executives from the reputed corporates and founders, established investors, and finalists.
